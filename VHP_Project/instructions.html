Instructions

-  Find and contact a veteran to interview. (How to locate a veteran)

-  Call or email the Vet to explain VHP and schedule a time/quiet place

o   During the call, get some background some information about the vet’s war(s), branch, rank, duties, etc.so you can do some background research

-  Search the web re the war and its location. (Background information on wars)

 

Prepare for interview

-  Get to know the VHP App and enter any new questions might have into script

o   Remember, you can skip any of the questions we suggested!

-  Practice using the App to conduct an interview and insert tags.

o   You’ll  need to tag at least 5 notable/interesting parts of the interview by tapping on the screen

 

On the day of the interview

-  Call or send an email/text reminder to the vet.

-  Make sure your phone is fully charged!

-  Bring tissues, notepaper, and a pen

 

Lights, camera…

-  Make sure your interview room is well-lit and very quiet

-  Remind vetsthey can skip any questions/can stop at any point

-  Set your phone on a tripod/leaning against something and put in airplane mode

-  Complete the THREE required forms.

o   Interviewer’s Release form—you

o   Biographical Data—vet

o   Veteran's Release—vet

-  Test, test--Check both sound and lighting

-  Sit close together and make sure the microphone is close to the vet

 

Action!

-  Start the recording and interview

-  Insert tags during the interview by tapping on screen

o   You’ll  need to tag at least 5 notable/interesting parts of the interview

o   To tag, simply tap on the screen. That will mark the to which you returnafter the interview to describe in required video recording log.

 

When interview is over

-  Thank vetand ask for any if they have any questions or concerns.

-  Ask the vet if they want to donate any original photos, letter, or other written material. Take pictures of any original photographs, letters, or written material

-  Take a photo of the veteran.

-  Take photo of any original photos, letters, or written material

-  Follow up promptly with a thank you email that includes your photos of their original photos, letters, and written materials and a link to the video.

 

Post production

-  Complete the required Video Recording log.

-  Upload the cover sheet, video, video recording log, and required forms (interviewer release form, veteran release form, and bio data form) to server.

-  If the vet donated any original materials, complete and upload the Photograph log and/or Manuscript data sheet.

Submit to the Library of Congress
