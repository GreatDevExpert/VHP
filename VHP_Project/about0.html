The Veterans History Project of the American Folklife Center collects, preserves, and makes accessible the personal accounts of American war veterans so that future generations may hear directly from veterans and better understand the realities of war.

The VHP’s extensive archives are preserved in the Library of Congress and made accessible through a searchable, online data base.

This App allows you to participate in the project using your smartphone.

It contains all the required forms, instructions, and tagging tools—making it easier for you to help preserve the stories of our nation’s veterans for ever.

For more information about the VHP please visit http://www.loc.gov/vets
